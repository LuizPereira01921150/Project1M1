  • GeoTagging

    This geotagging app allows users to add and manage location-based tags displayed directly on a map. Each tag is represented by a 10km radius circle, and the app alerts users if two tags overlap within that distance.

  • Sokoban

    This project is a mobile clone of the classic puzzle game Sokoban, where players push boxes onto designated target spots within a grid. The app challenges players to plan their moves carefully, providing multiple levels of increasing difficulty.

  • Lights-Out

    Lights-Out is a mobile adaptation of the classic puzzle game, challenging players to turn off all the lights on a grid by strategically tapping them. Each tap toggles the state of the selected light and its adjacent lights, requiring careful planning and logical thinking.

  • Guessing Game

    This game challenges players to listen to a song and guess its beats per minute (BPM). Players receive immediate feedback on their guesses, encouraging quick thinking and rhythm recognition.

  • PhotoMagic

    PhotoMagic is an app that securely encodes images using a fibLFSR (Fibonacci Linear Feedback Shift Register) encoder and decodes them with a corresponding decryption key. The app explores steganography and cryptography concepts, allowing users to hide and reveal information within images.
